About

I am an Algorithm Engineer at Doubao Team, ByteDance, where I am primarily responsible for the alignment of the Doubao series models during the post-training phase. I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Zhoujun Li. I also received my B.S. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University in 2019.

My research focuses on advancing the capabilities of large language models, with particular emphasis on model alignment, document intelligence, and the development of AI agents. I have published several papers in top-tier conferences including EMNLP, AAAI, KDD, and COLING, contributing to the fields of natural language processing and machine learning.
